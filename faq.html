Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning with Detmax.

1. General Information

Q1: What courses do you offer?

We offer professional driving courses (both manual and automatic) and a variety of computer training programs, including basic computer skills, packages, and advanced courses.

Q2: Where are you located?

Detmax Driving School and Computer College operates from accessible training centers. Please contact us for the nearest branch location.

Q3: How do I contact Detmax?

Phone: 0114971070 / 0111379171
Website: detmaxinstitute.co.ke

2. Enrollment

Q4: How do I enroll?

You can enroll by visiting our office, contacting us via phone, or registering through our website.

Q5: What documents are required for enrollment?
  • National ID or Passport
  • Passport-size photos
  • Any required academic documents (for computer courses)
Q6: Is there an age requirement for driving courses?

Yes, students must meet the legal minimum driving age as required by law.

3. Fees and Payments

Q7: How much are the course fees?

Fees vary depending on the course. Please contact us for the latest pricing and available discounts.

Q8: Do you offer discounts?

Yes, we occasionally offer promotions (e.g., student discounts). Terms apply.

Q9: Are fees refundable?

Fees are generally non-refundable, except under special circumstances approved by management.

4. Driving Courses

Q10: Do you offer both manual and automatic driving lessons?

Yes, we provide training for both manual and automatic vehicles.

Q11: How long does the driving course take?

The duration depends on the package selected and the learner’s progress.

Q12: Do you guarantee a driving license after training?

No, we prepare you thoroughly, but passing the official driving test depends on your performance.

Q13: Are the training vehicles safe?

Yes, all our vehicles are regularly maintained and meet safety standards.

5. Computer Courses

Q14: What computer courses are available?

We offer beginner to advanced programs, including computer basics, Microsoft Office packages, internet skills, and more.

Q15: Do I need prior computer knowledge?

No, we have beginner-friendly courses for those with no prior experience.

Q16: Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, certificates are issued upon successful completion of the course.

6. Training Schedule

Q17: Do you offer flexible class schedules?

Yes, we provide flexible schedules, including weekday and weekend classes.

Q18: Can I reschedule my classes?

Yes, rescheduling is allowed with prior notice, subject to availability.

7. Student Support

Q19: Do you provide support during training?

Yes, our instructors and staff are always available to guide and support you.

Q20: What happens if I miss a class?

You should inform the administration in advance. Makeup sessions may be arranged where possible.

8. Safety & Conduct

Q21: What safety measures are in place during driving lessons?

We follow strict safety protocols, including instructor supervision and well-maintained vehicles.

Q22: What behavior is expected from students?

Students are expected to be respectful, disciplined, and follow all institutional rules.

9. Certification & Completion

Q23: What do I need to complete my course?

You must attend required sessions, meet performance standards, and pass assessments.

Q24: How long does it take to receive a certificate?

Certificates are issued within a reasonable time after successful completion.

10. Other Questions

Q25: Can I enroll in more than one course?

Yes, you can enroll in multiple courses depending on your schedule.

Q26: Do you offer job placement assistance?

We may provide guidance and recommendations, but job placement is not guaranteed.
