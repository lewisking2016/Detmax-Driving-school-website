Our Story & Mission

Our Foundation

Detmax Driving School and Computer College was established in 2022 with a clear vision: to provide quality, accessible education to Kenyans from all walks of life. Registered under Detmax Limited Company (PIN: P051811093F), we started with driving school and have expanded to offer online computer programs to reach students across the country.

We are in collaboration with PMAK (Peer Mentors Association of Kenya) to reach communities and provide training opportunities to those who need them most.

Our Vision

"To be the leading training center with excellent output in both moral and professional qualification."

Our Mission

"To provide a unique, cost-efficient, and mobile driving school and computer college by reaching out to the grassroots level."
